Memorandum of Understanding Between W3C and WHATWG万维网联盟与WHATWG之间的谅解备忘录

This Memorandum of Understanding (“MOU ”) is entered into this __May 28, 2019, between the World Wide Web Consortium (“W3C”), with its principal place of business located at 32 Vassar Street, Room 32-386, Cambridge, MA, 02139 USA; and the WHATWG. 本谅解备忘录（"MOU"）于2019年5月28日在万维网联盟（"W3C"）和WHATWG之间签订，W3C的主要办公地点位于32 Vassar Street, Room 32-386, Cambridge, MA, 02139 USA。

Whereas W3C is an international community where Member organizations, a full-time staff, and the public work together to develop Web Standards. W3C’s mission is to lead the Web to its full potential. 鉴于W3C是一个国际社区，其成员组织、全职工作人员和公众共同致力于开发网络标准。W3C的使命是引导网络发挥其全部潜力。

Whereas WHATWG is a community of people interested in evolving the web through standards and tests and working together to create standards implementable in web browsers; 鉴于WHATWG是一个由对通过标准和测试来发展网络感兴趣的人组成的社区，并共同致力于创建可在网络浏览器中实施的标准。

Whereas W3C and WHATWG both contribute to the development of the Web; 鉴于W3C和WHATWG都对网络的发展做出了贡献。

Whereas W3C and WHATWG want to work together to advance the open Web platform; 鉴于W3C和WHATWG希望共同推进开放网络平台的发展。

Whereas W3C and WHATWG believe that the Web community is best served by a single development stream for each of the Web’s specifications, due to the high costs of specification divergence and of focusing parts of the community on different documents; 鉴于W3C和WHATWG认为，由于规范的分歧和将社区的部分成员集中在不同的文件上的成本很高，因此，Web社区最好由一个单一的开发流来完成每个Web的规范。

Whereas W3C and WHATWG want to provide an environment in which all of the Web’s stakeholders can communicate their needs, interests, and interoperability considerations; 鉴于W3C和WHATWG希望提供一个环境，使Web的所有利益相关者能够交流他们的需求、利益和互操作性的考虑。

Whereas W3C and WHATWG want to continue to enhance the development of the Web platform as interoperable, royalty-free, globally available, accessible to all, secure, and private. 鉴于W3C和WHATWG希望继续加强Web平台的发展，使其成为可互操作的、免版税的、全球通用的、所有人都能访问的、安全的和私有的。

Now, therefore, in furtherance of the desire to voluntarily cooperate with each other on the processes, mechanisms, and plans set forth in this MOU, W3C and WHATWG (“Parties”) agree as follows: 因此，为了进一步推动自愿就本谅解备忘录中规定的进程、机制和计划相互合作的愿望，W3C和WHATWG（"各方"）达成如下协议。

This MOU describes a collaboration process for the development of the HTML and DOM specifications published (in the past or future) by both W3C and WHATWG, where such specifications include specifications that are in the WHATWG versions of HTML and DOM but have been published separately at W3C. This MOU also sets forth certain publication mechanisms for the Parties around specifications published by W3C or WHATWG, and a transition plan for the W3C around the listed specifications. The Parties may expand the scope of the collaboration process set forth in this MOU beyond the HTML and DOM specifications only by a subsequent MOU that sets forth such expanded scope. 本谅解备忘录描述了W3C和WHATWG双方（在过去或未来）发布的HTML和DOM规范的合作过程，其中这些规范包括WHATWG版本的HTML和DOM中的规范，但在W3C已经单独发布。本谅解备忘录还规定了双方围绕W3C或WHATWG发布的规范的某些发布机制，以及W3C围绕所列规范的过渡计划。双方可以将本谅解备忘录中规定的合作程序的范围扩大到HTML和DOM规范之外，但必须通过随后的谅解备忘录来规定这种扩大的范围。

Except for the terms set forth in (i) this paragraph and (ii) the “Term and Termination”, “Limitation of Liability”, “Choice of Law and Venue”, “Complete Agreement”, “Independent Parties”, “No Waiver; Severability”, and “Notices” sections below ((i) and (ii) collectively, the “Binding Commitments”), the terms of this MOU reflect only a voluntary statement of intent by the Parties to engage in the proposed collaboration process described further below. Any and all terms that are not Binding Commitments (“Non-Binding Commitments”) do not constitute a binding agreement between the Parties in any respect. 除了(i)本段和(ii) "期限和终止"、"责任限制"、"法律和地点选择"、"完整协议"、"独立方"、"不放弃；可分割性 "和 "通知 "部分（(i)和(ii)统称为 "约束性承诺"）中规定的条款，本谅解备忘录的条款仅反映双方对参与下文所述拟议合作进程意向的自愿声明。任何及所有非约束性承诺的条款（"非约束性承诺"）在任何方面都不构成双方之间有约束力的协议。

1 Plan for HTML and DOM specifications1 关于HTML和DOM规范的计划

2 HTML and DOM shall be developed principally in the WHATWG, following WHATWG Living Standard (LS) specification process. W3C intends to give input to and endorse WHATWG Review Drafts to become W3C Recommendations, through the W3C Process from Candidate Recommendation (CR) through Proposed Recommendation (PR) to Recommendation (REC). Our Design Goal is that the W3C CR, PR, and REC, and the WHATWG Review Draft are the same document. 2 HTML和DOM主要由WHATWG开发，遵循WHATWG生活标准（LS）规范流程。W3C打算通过W3C的流程，从候选建议(CR)到拟议建议(PR)再到建议(REC)，为WHATWG审查草案提供意见并认可其成为W3C建议。我们的设计目标是，W3C的CR、PR和REC，以及WHATWG的Review Draft是同一个文件。

3 Cooperation Process3 合作过程

  1. W3C shall propose to its Membership to charter a Working Group, referred to herein as the “HTML Working Group” (HTML WG, although it may take a different name). The HTML WG charter will be to assist the W3C community in raising issues and proposing solutions for the HTML and DOM specifications, and to bring Review Drafts to Recommendation. W3C将向其成员提议成立一个工作组，在此称为 "HTML工作组"(HTML WG，尽管它可能采用不同的名称)。HTML工作组的任务是协助W3C社区为HTML和DOM规范提出问题和解决方案，并将审查稿提交给建议。
  2. The discussions of the HTML WG should ultimately be reflected in the WHATWG GitHub repositories, with the aim that issues be resolved in the WHATWG working mode. In the event that a person raising an issue feels that the issue has not been fairly resolved by WHATWG, the HTML WG may help to explain the resolution. To achieve this, the WG may have meetings, repositories, etc. in which they engage the community. While the HTML WG attempts to work with the WHATWG editors to achieve consensus, there may be times that the HTML WG and the WHATWG editors are unable to find consensus. They shall then engage in the conflict resolution process detailed below. HTML工作组的讨论最终应该反映在WHATWG的GitHub仓库中，目的是使问题在WHATWG的工作模式中得到解决。如果提出问题的人认为该问题没有得到WHATWG的公平解决，HTML工作组可以帮助解释该决议。为了达到这个目的，工作组可能会有会议、资料库等，让社区参与其中。虽然HTML工作组试图与WHATWG的编辑们一起工作以达成共识，但有时HTML工作组和WHATWG的编辑们可能无法找到共识。这时，他们应参与下面详述的冲突解决过程。
  3. W3C shall encourage the community (users and developers alike) and horizontal review groups to contribute directly to the WHATWG HTML and DOM repositories; raising issues, proposing solutions, commenting on proposed solutions, and indicating support or otherwise for proposals. Those contributions will be subject to the WHATWG IPR Policy (see “Patent and Copyright”, below). The WHATWG repositories may add custom labels to enable the HTML WG to organize and manage issues of interest to the W3C community. W3C将鼓励社区（用户和开发者）和横向审查小组直接对WHATWG HTML和DOM库做出贡献；提出问题，建议解决方案，对建议的解决方案进行评论，并表示支持或不支持建议。这些贡献将受到WHATWG知识产权政策的约束（见下文 "专利和版权"）。WHATWG资源库可以添加自定义标签，以使HTML工作组能够组织和管理W3C社区感兴趣的问题。
  4. When WHATWG publishes a Review Draft of its HTML or DOM Living Standards, W3C may endorse this Review Draft as a CR, PR, and/or REC, with both W3C and WHATWG logos, per the following procedure: 当WHATWG发布其HTML或DOM生活标准的Review Draft时，W3C可以根据以下程序认可该Review Draft为CR、PR和/或REC，并带有W3C和WHATWG的标志。
    1. The HTML WG works to demonstrate to the Director that the contents of the WHATWG Review Draft have had wide review, issues have been addressed, and the contents have sufficient CR exit criteria as defined by W3C Process. The HTML WG will look for a consensus of its participants to advance each Review Draft as a CR and will have worked to engage the community to make this consensus achievable. HTML工作组努力向主任证明，WHATWG Review Draft的内容已经过广泛的审查，问题已经得到解决，并且内容具有W3C流程所定义的足够的CR退出标准。HTML工作组将寻求其参与者达成共识，将每个Review Draft推进为CR，并努力让社区参与进来，以使这种共识得以实现。
    2. This document then goes through the REC process including AC review, and if there are no objections, becomes a W3C Recommendation. At each stage (CR, PR, REC) of this process, WHATWG would add the W3C's endorsement to the appropriate Review Draft, in a prominent place, including a W3C logo and stating that the Review Draft is a W3C CR, PR, and/or REC. 这个文件然后经过REC过程，包括AC审查，如果没有反对意见，就成为W3C建议。在这个过程的每个阶段（CR、PR、REC），WHATWG都会将W3C的认可添加到相应的Review Draft中，并放在明显的位置，包括W3C的标志，并说明该Review Draft是W3C的CR、PR和/或REC。
    3. Multiple endorsements will be added to the same document rather than creating separate documents for each stage. 多个认可将被添加到同一个文件中，而不是为每个阶段创建单独的文件。
    4. As the W3C process requires implementation experience to advance beyond CR, while WHATWG process requires only implementation commitments, Review Drafts will include feature implementation status annotations in the margin. The granularity of the annotations should be such that they can be reasonable indicators of which features have implementation experience. The status indicators will be sourced from an external site or set of test cases, such as caniuse or wpt.fyi. The CR endorsement will mention that all features that do not have two implementations according to the annotations are at risk. The PR and REC endorsement will mention that all features which do not have two implementations are considered informative, not normative, for W3C PR/REC purposes. The necessary annotations already exist in the HTML Living Standard, and the mechanism will be added to the DOM Living Standard. WHATWG will welcome pull requests to add further annotations as needed. WHATWG will not add manually managed or finer-grained annotations for this purpose. W3C and WHATWG will collaborate on tooling to enhance the annotations. 由于W3C流程要求有实施经验才能超越CR，而WHATWG流程只要求有实施承诺，Review Drafts将在空白处包括功能实施状态注释。注释的颗粒度应该是能够合理地显示哪些功能有实施经验。这些状态指标将来自外部网站或测试案例集，如caniuse或wpt.fyi。CR认可将提到，所有没有两个实现的特性根据注释都是有风险的。PR和REC认可将提到所有没有两个实现的特性被认为是信息性的，而不是规范性的，用于W3C PR/REC目的。必要的注释已经存在于HTML生活标准中，并且该机制将被添加到DOM生活标准中。WHATWG将欢迎拉动请求，以添加更多的注释，如果需要的话。WHATWG将不会为此目的添加人工管理的或更精细的注释。W3C和WHATWG将在工具方面进行合作，以加强注释。
  5. W3C may charter Working Groups to develop extensions to HTML. WHATWG agrees to work with W3C to define appropriate extension points in HTML or DOM. Issues with or enhancements to those extension points shall be discussed in the WHATWG HTML and DOM repositories and with the W3C TAG. W3C可能会成立工作小组来开发HTML的扩展。WHATWG同意与W3C合作，在HTML或DOM中定义适当的扩展点。对这些扩展点的问题或改进应在WHATWG的HTML和DOM库中讨论，并与W3C的TAG讨论。
  6. W3C and WHATWG shall continue to use and maintain web-platform-tests (WPT) as the testing infrastructure for the platform. W3C和WHATWG将继续使用和维护网络平台测试（WPT）作为平台的测试基础设施。
  7. W3C and WHATWG jointly agree that the TAG may provide non-binding technical advice on any complex disagreements. W3C和WHATWG共同同意，TAG可以就任何复杂的分歧提供不具约束力的技术建议。
  8. W3C has no obligation to bring every Review Draft to REC, but expects to do so at least once per 12 month period. Consistent with WHATWG’s policies and normal course of operation, a new WHATWG Review Draft will be published approximately every six months. The HTML WG can take the newly published Review Drafts for the HTML and DOM specifications to REC. The HTML WG may request that WHATWG publish a WHATWG Review Draft outside the normal course of operation; WHATWG will make reasonable efforts to produce such Review Draft. W3C没有义务向REC提交每个Review Draft，但希望每12个月至少有一次。与WHATWG的政策和正常运作过程一致，新的WHATWG Review Draft将大约每6个月发布一次。HTML工作组可以将新发布的HTML和DOM规范的Review Drafts提交给REC。HTML工作组可以要求WHATWG在正常的运作过程之外发布一个WHATWG Review Draft；WHATWG将做出合理的努力来制作这样的Review Draft。
  9. The Cooperation Process above addresses the anticipated common case, in which the WHATWG Review Draft meets W3C REC criteria and there are no objections. The Parties agree to the following exception process to deal with conflicts and lack of agreement. 上面的合作程序解决了预期的常见情况，即WHATWG Review Draft符合W3C REC的标准并且没有反对意见。双方同意采用以下例外程序来处理冲突和缺乏协议的情况。

4 Conflict Resolution4 冲突的解决

  1. When there is an unresolved objection, all Parties agree to make substantial effort to resolve the conflict so that W3C can publish a REC with no normative differences from the WHATWG Review Draft of an HTML or DOM specification. If the HTML WG cannot reach consensus to bring a desired Review Draft forward on the REC track, it must ensure that appropriate issues are raised or encourage the editor to re-open issues in the WHATWG repository. 当有一个未解决的反对意见时，所有各方都同意做出实质性的努力来解决这个冲突，以便W3C能够发布一个与HTML或DOM规范的WHATWG Review Draft没有规范性差异的REC。如果HTML工作组不能达成共识，在REC的轨道上推进所需的Review Draft，它必须确保提出适当的问题，或鼓励编辑在WHATWG资源库中重开问题。
  2. The Parties and participants agree to take the following escalation steps, each step to be reached only once the preceding steps have been given good faith efforts to reach consensus: 各方和参与者同意采取以下升级步骤，每一步都要在前面的步骤得到真诚的努力以达成共识后才能达到。
    1. The HTML WG shall attempt to resolve the objection, working to explain its members’ concerns to the WHATWG editors and other Workstream participants (through their GitHub repository) and to explain the decision of the WHATWG to those raising the concerns; HTML工作组应尝试解决异议，努力向WHATWG编辑和其他工作流参与者（通过他们的GitHub仓库）解释其成员的关切，并向提出关切的人解释WHATWG的决定。
    2. The HTML WG shall escalate the disagreement to the WHATWG Steering Group to help resolve the issue; HTML工作组应将分歧上报给WHATWG指导小组，以帮助解决这个问题。
    3. The HTML WG shall explore techniques other than having a HTML or DOM specification with normative differences such as an extension specification; or as non-normative advice; HTML工作组应探索除了拥有HTML或DOM规范以外的技术，如扩展规范；或作为非规范性建议。
    4. The HTML WG, WHATWG Steering Group, an individual objector, or W3C Groups with dependencies on WHATWG specifications, including horizontal review responsibilities, may request technical advice from the TAG. The TAG may consult other experts, including for expertise not currently available on the TAG. The TAG may be able to find some consensus or explain to one side or the other any negative impacts of their position; HTML工作组、WHATWG指导小组、个别反对者、或对WHATWG规范有依赖性的W3C小组，包括横向审查责任，可以要求TAG提供技术建议。技术咨询小组可以咨询其他专家，包括咨询技术咨询小组目前不具备的专业知识。技术咨询小组可能会找到一些共识，或者向一方或另一方解释其立场的任何负面影响。
    5. The issue shall go to the W3C Director, who examines the arguments using the record of the tracked issue, the HTML WG analysis, and the TAG analysis. 该问题应提交给W3C主任，主任将利用跟踪问题的记录、HTML工作组的分析和TAG的分析来审查这些论点。
      1. If the Director overrules the objections, then W3C continues its process to publish the HTML or DOM Review Draft as a REC; 如果主任驳回了反对意见，那么W3C将继续其发布HTML或DOM审查稿的过程，作为REC。
      2. If the Director sustains the objection, the Director and/or the TAG may choose to re-open the appeal to the WHATWG Steering Group with any new data or rationale; 如果主任支持反对意见，主任和/或TAG可以选择以任何新的数据或理由向WHATWG指导小组重新提出上诉。
  3. The W3C may choose to: W3C可以选择
    1. decline to endorse a particular Review Draft as a Recommendation and continue the discussion for the next Review Draft; or 拒绝批准某一特定的审查草案作为建议，并继续讨论下一个审查草案；或
    2. if the differences are not too entangled with the rest of the spec, the REC might be issued as the Review Draft plus a companion document. 如果与规范的其他部分没有太多纠葛，REC可以作为Review Draft和一个配套文件发布。
  4. Finally, if W3C management and the WHATWG Steering Group cannot reach agreement on a single HTML or DOM standard through direct negotiations, W3C may (under rights granted by the Creative Commons Attribution International 4.0 (CC BY 4.0) copyright license, or a successor copyright license that is at least as permissive, as specified in the WHATWG Intellectual Property Rights policy) publish a Recommendation with differences, and WHATWG may terminate this MOU. 最后，如果W3C管理层和WHATWG指导小组不能通过直接谈判就单一的HTML或DOM标准达成协议，W3C可以（根据知识共享署名国际4.0（CC BY 4.0）版权许可授予的权利，或根据WHATWG知识产权政策规定的至少是同样许可的后续版权许可）发布一个有分歧的建议，而WHATWG可以终止本谅解备忘录。

5 Format of jointly published documents5 联合发表的文件的格式

The documents that are both WHATWG Review Drafts and W3C CR, PR, or RECs shall: 既是WHATWG Review Drafts又是W3C CR、PR或REC的文件应

  1. Be published on whatwg.org; 发布在whatwg.org上。

    To protect reader expectations of privacy, if whatwg.org does not have a privacy policy that meets industry norms, W3C may change the /TR links to point to self-hosted copies. This provision will not take effect until 6 months after the effective date of this MOU. 为了保护读者对隐私的期望，如果whatwg.org没有符合行业规范的隐私政策，W3C可能会改变/TR链接以指向自我托管的副本。这项规定在本谅解备忘录生效6个月后才会生效。

  2. Be backed up by W3C for assurance of persistence (these backups will not be publicly accessible); 由W3C进行备份，以保证其持久性（这些备份将不会被公开访问）。
  3. Be accessible via a URL on w3.org that returns a 302 redirect to the URL on whatwg.org; 可通过w3.org上的一个URL访问，该URL会返回到whatwg.org上的302重定向。
  4. Indicate that copyright is jointly owned by W3C and WHATWG, indicating WHATWG and the WHATWG Steering Group Members; 标明版权由W3C和WHATWG共同拥有，表明WHATWG和WHATWG指导小组成员。
  5. Make reasonable efforts to conform to WCAG Level AA, by using appropriate tools and styles and correcting Level AA conformance issues when identified; 通过使用适当的工具和样式，并在发现AA级别的一致性问题时予以纠正，做出合理的努力以符合WCAG AA级别。
  6. Identify “At Risk” features at CR, via enumeration and a statement that features not demonstrating sufficient implementation experience are at risk; 通过列举和说明没有表现出足够的实施经验的功能是有风险的，来确定CR的 "风险 "功能。
  7. Be identified as one or more of CR, PR, or REC (with different dates for reaching the different statuses), as appropriate; 酌情确定为CR、PR或REC中的一个或多个（达到不同状态的日期不同）。
  8. Be published in a mutually agreed style, as shown in Appendix 1; 以双方同意的风格发布，如附录1所示。
  9. Include a logo for each organization; and 包括每个组织的标识；以及
  10. Display a status box as in Appendix 1, once approved as a W3C CR, PR, or REC. 一旦被批准为W3C的CR、PR或REC，则显示附录1中的状态框。

6 Normative referencing6 规范性引用

Normative references from W3C specifications to material that is in the HTML and DOM specifications shall reference the whatwg.org Living Standard both in-line and in the Normative References section. W3C advises that W3C specifications also reference the W3C Recommendation in the References section. W3C规范中对HTML和DOM规范中的材料的规范性引用应在行内和规范性引用部分引用whatwg.org生活标准。W3C建议，W3C规范也应在参考文献部分引用W3C建议。

7 Error management7 错误管理

  1. WHATWG does not provide for an errata process. The expectation is that typographical errors and mistakes will be fixed in the Living Standard through its usual process, and will then appear in the W3C version at the next WHATWG Review Draft that becomes a W3C Recommendation. WHATWG没有规定勘误程序。我们的期望是，印刷错误和错误将通过通常的程序在生活标准中得到修正，然后在下一个成为W3C建议的WHATWG审查草案中出现在W3C版本中。
  2. To allow for more urgent errata on a specific WHATWG Review Draft/W3C Recommendation, W3C will provide an errata page, linked from the W3C Recommendation Status section. All urgent errata will link to the relevant pull request in the WHATWG repository, and summarize the status of the pull request. 为了允许对特定的WHATWG Review Draft/W3C Recommendation进行更紧急的勘误，W3C将提供一个勘误页面，从W3C Recommendation状态部分链接。所有紧急勘误将链接到WHATWG资源库中的相关拉动请求，并总结拉动请求的状态。

8 Transition Plan8 过渡计划

  1. W3C agrees to discontinue its release plans for W3C versions of HTML 5.3 and DOM 4.1. W3C同意停止其对HTML 5.3和DOM 4.1的W3C版本的发布计划。

10 Forking10 分叉

  1. While the copyright licenses of both Parties’ documents permit copying to create derivative works with appropriate attribution, a goal of this MOU is to avoid such copying for the purpose of specifications. 虽然双方文件的版权许可允许复制以创造衍生作品，但本谅解备忘录的一个目标是避免以规范为目的的这种复制。
  2. If Parties agree that text should be moved or copied from a deliverable originally published by one to a deliverable published by the other, or that an entire deliverable should be moved across group boundaries, they may mutually agree to do so within the bounds of this MOU. Movement or copying text from incubation-track or non-standards track deliverables shall also be acceptable. 如果双方同意将最初由一方发布的交付物中的文本移动或复制到另一方发布的交付物中，或将整个交付物跨组移动，他们可以在本谅解备忘录的范围内相互同意这样做。从孵化轨道或非标准轨道交付品中移动或复制文本也应被接受。

In the event that good faith negotiation fails to reach agreement, then termination of this MOU may be permitted as follows: 如果一方对拟议的或实际的文本复制或衍生文件的出版有异议，那么WHATWG SG和W3C管理层将试图通过谈判来解决。如果真诚的谈判未能达成协议，那么可以允许终止本谅解备忘录，具体如下。
